EZAdsPro

My experience at EZAdsPro included working very closely with both team members and clients. This included designing advertisements for both web and print, communicating with both clients and the in-house team, as well as finalizing print materials for production purposes.

Our Sunday Visitor

During my time at Our Sunday Visitor, Inc. I worked very closely with the marketing team. This included various jobs such as designing business to business promotional sales pieces, pamphlets and ads for books, third-party ads and more for both print and digital channels.

Elemental Supplements

Being healthy can be achieved in many ways but most of them revolve around the idea of nutrition. When looking into this, it is shown that eating whole foods and staying away from heavily processed items is most beneficial. It seems that every product available on the market doesn't reflect this idea. The concept of Elemental Real Food Supplements is to bridge that gap and to get as close to natural whole foods as you possibly can with supplements.

YumYum Boards

Skateboards have always had a draw to me. After attempting, and failing, to learn to ride them I gave up but just couldn't get rid of them. I realized that the designs each had were specific and reflective of the idividual who rode it. I enjoyed looking through each and realized that board art could be a great outlet for my illustrative, less refined and polished side of design. YumYum Boards is a collection of some of those designs as well as a branding project.
